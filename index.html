Steady

Audiobook + Steady background noise. For your bluetooth, your batteries, and your brain.

Now playing

The big button plays/pauses the audiobook and the background noise together — pausing stops both, so your headphones can sleep. The triangles jump back/forward (single = 15s, double = 60s). The chapter buttons change chapter — and for 30 seconds after, tapping the opposite one undoes an accidental change and puts you back where you were. The top bar shows your place in the whole book; the lower bar scrubs the current chapter. Tap the cover to change the picture.

Background noise

Levels are matched to the audiobook, so 100% noise ≈ book loudness — keep it low, just enough to fill the silences. Brown is the deepest/rumbliest, white the hissiest. Tap ＋ to add your own looping noise file; it'll be normalized to the same scale.

Bookshelf

Add a whole book folder (sub-folders and cover art are pulled in automatically) or loose files. Tap a book to open it — each one remembers where you left off. Drag ⠿ to reorder, ✎ to rename, ✕ to remove. Export backup saves everything (books + your place) to a file you can move to another device and Import there.
  • No books yet. Tap “Add a book” and pick a book’s folder. Add as many as you like — each one remembers where you left off.
